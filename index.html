Frequently Asked Questions

What are the system requirements?

You need a computer running Windows 10 or 11 to act as the server, an 80mm thermal receipt printer (ESC/POS compatible), and any modern device with a web browser (like a tablet, phone, or laptop) on the same Wi-Fi network.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All your data—including your menu and daily sales logs—is stored locally on your own computer. It is never sent to the cloud or accessible by anyone else, giving you complete privacy and control.

What kind of support is included?

Your purchase includes email support for installation and configuration, as well as access to all future software updates which often include bug fixes and new features.
